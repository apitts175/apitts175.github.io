How I Have Given Back to The Community

I grew up in Friendswood, TX, which is very close to Galveston. I lived through and experienced the brunt of Hurricane Harvey . It even flooded our house. In the midst and aftermath, I was a volunteer in wreckage cleanup and worked in meal preperation for those in need of relief.

I consider myself to be environmentally conscious and volunteered all 4 years in high school being a part of the Recycling Club, where we managed and collected all of the recycling bins throughout the school, and cleaned up litter from the nearby park.

Frequent volunteering at my Church's various miscellaneous activites, usually canned food drives and working in a volunteer soup kitchen.
